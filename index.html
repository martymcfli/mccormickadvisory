The Founder

Owen McCormick

"I didn't come to cannabis consulting from a PowerPoint deck. I came from running a compliant operation in one of the most scrutinized markets in the country — and selling it."

Owen McCormick founded and operated Trail Hazers LLC, a fully compliant I-71 gifting operation in Washington D.C. that scaled to $2.56 million in revenue before a successful exit. That journey — from first SOP to final close — is the foundation of everything McCormick Advisory does.

Unlike consultants who've only theorized about cannabis operations, Owen has negotiated vendor relationships, managed compliance audits, built a team, run marketing campaigns, and navigated the specific legal ambiguities of a gifting-model market. He's been the operator who had to make every decision work in the real world.

Today he brings that operator lens together with formal expertise in revenue operations, growth systems, and process design — helping cannabis businesses build the infrastructure that makes them scalable, sellable, and defensible. He works with a select number of clients at a time, based between New York City and San Antonio.
